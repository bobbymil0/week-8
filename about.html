Our Story

From Tuscany to San Francisco

Bella Vita was founded in 2005 by the Rossi family, who brought their culinary heritage from a small village in Tuscany to the heart of San Francisco. What began as a small, family-run trattoria has grown into one of the city's most beloved Italian restaurants.

Our philosophy is simple: use the finest ingredients, honor traditional recipes, and create a warm, welcoming atmosphere where guests feel like family. We believe that the best Italian food comes from a perfect balance of tradition and passion.

Each dish at Bella Vita tells a story - of our heritage, our commitment to quality, and our love for sharing authentic Italian cuisine with our community.
